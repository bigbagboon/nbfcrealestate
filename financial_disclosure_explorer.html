Comprehensive statutory, accounting, and supervisory mandates for listed Non-Banking Financial Companies under Schedule III Division III and RBI SBR.

Statutory Context: Division III

Listed NBFCs must prioritize Liquidity-based presentation. Unlike traditional firms, assets and liabilities are bifurcated into Financial and Non-Financial categories on the face of the balance sheet. This structure highlights the intermediation role of financial institutions.

Financial Ratio Mandates

Companies must explain variances >25% compared to the previous year. NBFCs (Division III) use liquidity-focused ratios, while Developers (Division II) use operational efficiency ratios.

Division III (NBFC)

  • Capital Adequacy (CRAR) Required
  • Tier I / Tier II Ratios Required
  • Liquidity Coverage (LCR) Required
  • Inventory Turnover N/A
  • Current Ratio N/A

Division II (Real Estate)

  • Current Ratio Required
  • Debt-Equity Ratio Required
  • Inventory Turnover Required
  • Debt Service Coverage (DSCR) Required
  • Net Profit Ratio Required
